===== Installation =====
* Follow instructions on [[https://www.jenkins.io/|Jenkins Website]]
* home : ''/var/lib/jenkins''
* Configutation files
	* server config: ''/etc/default/jenkins''
	* jenkins settings: ''/var/lib/jenkins/config.xml''
* systemd service: ''jenkins''
